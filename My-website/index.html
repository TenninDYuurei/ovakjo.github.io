About me

Ovakporaye Joseph Divine Oghenedoro was born on the 13th of July, 2002, at the university of Port-Harcourt Teaching Hospital (UPTH).
He is an indigene of orogun, Ughelli-North in Delta State, and currently resides in Port-Harcourt.

Education

He attended the Gifted Hands primary school, graduated in the year 2013 and went ahead to complete his secondary school education at the Federal Government College Port-Harcourt, which he graduated from in the year 2019.
He is currently studying pharmacy at the University of Port-Harcourt.

Other Information

Work

He is a copywriter, a digital marketer, a forex trader, a fullstack developer etc.
He is currently a freelancer at Upwork, Freelancer and Fiverr, and does remote work on LinkedIn.

Hobbies
